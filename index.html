What is Recaptain?


Recaptain is a bot that recaps messages in any public slack channel.

Created so users don't have to search through messages

Let Recaptain do that for you!

Forgetting Something?

Recaptain can recap any public channel. With built in keyword functionality, never miss another important message!

Powered By IBM Watson

Don't know any keywords to search? No problem!

Recaptain also uses Watsons Language API to find the most important keywords and messages that you've missed.

Easy and Versitile Usage


Recaptain allows you to recap mutliple channels at once.

Specify how far back you want your recap, going as far back as your slack message history will allow.

Never miss another important message

How to Set-up Recaptain

Start by going to Slack channel and going to your #General channel

After that you'll want to go to the settings button by the search bar at top, and click "Add an app or integration"

Next, go to the top right corner and click on the Build button located by your team name.

After that you'll want to click "Make a Custom Integration"

Then, click on Bots and fill in a username for the bot (Recaptain)

Finally, take the API token and copy/paste it into our form right here on the site!
